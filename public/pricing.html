Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?

Yes. Your subscription runs for 30 days from purchase. You won't be charged again unless you renew. There are no cancellation fees.

What happens to my data if I downgrade?

All your food logs, order history, and earned coins are preserved. You simply lose access to Pro-only features like unlimited AI chat.

Is the Free plan actually free forever?

Yes — no credit card required. The Free plan is permanently free with no hidden charges.

How do I pay?
