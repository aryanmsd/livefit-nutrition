Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data.

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use LiveFit, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: username, email address, and hashed password when you register.
  • Food logs: meal entries, food names, and nutritional data you record.
  • Order data: meal orders, payment confirmations, and invoices.
  • Usage data: coins balance, rewards redeemed, and subscription status.
  • Device data: basic platform information when using the mobile app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the LiveFit nutrition tracking service.
  • To process meal orders and generate invoices.
  • To power the AI Nutrition Coach with your today's intake context.
  • To manage your coins, rewards, and subscription.
  • To send password reset emails when requested.
  • To communicate important service updates.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in MongoDB Atlas with encryption at rest. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text. All API communication uses HTTPS. Authentication tokens (JWT) expire after 7 days.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We only share data with:

  • Razorpay — for payment processing (they have their own privacy policy).
  • MongoDB Atlas — for secure cloud database storage.
  • Law enforcement, only when legally required.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To request deletion of your account and all associated data, contact us at nutrition.livefit@gmail.com.

6. Cookies

LiveFit uses localStorage (not cookies) to store your authentication token and username on your device. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of LiveFit after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, email us at nutrition.livefit@gmail.com.
