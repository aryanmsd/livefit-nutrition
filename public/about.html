About LiveFit

We're on a mission to make nutrition tracking effortless, intelligent, and actually enjoyable.

Our Story

LiveFit was born out of frustration with complicated, joyless nutrition apps. We believed tracking your food should feel empowering, not punishing. Founded in 2024, we set out to build a platform that combines AI-powered food identification, real-time nutrition insights, and a rewarding experience — all in one place.

Our Mission

To democratise personalised nutrition — giving every person the tools, knowledge, and motivation to eat well and feel great, regardless of their fitness level or background. We use cutting-edge AI to identify food from photos, track macros in real time, and deliver actionable insights that actually fit your life.
